Why use this module?

Standard sales flows often require product availability to depend on a customer, project, region or pricelist. This module adds a practical and maintainable way to keep product selection under control.

1

Prevent wrong products

Sales users only see products that are valid for the selected pricelist, reducing mistakes in quotations.

2

Manage by groups

Group products into Product Groups and allow those groups per pricelist for clean catalog control.

3

Handle exceptions

Block specific products on a pricelist or exclude specific pricelists directly from a product.

Main features

Everything needed to manage pricelist-based product restrictions in Odoo Sales.

Product Groups on products

Assign one or more project/product groups to product templates and use those groups to control product availability.

Allowed Product Groups on pricelists

Define which groups are allowed for each pricelist and filter the sales order product selector accordingly.

Forbidden Products on pricelists

Explicitly block individual products on a pricelist, even if the product group would normally be allowed.

Excluded Pricelists on products

Configure on a product which pricelists should never allow that product to be selected.

Automatic cleanup

Invalid order lines can be removed automatically or blocked, depending on the selected policy.

Optional product support

Optional products remain usable when they are linked to valid parent products and are not explicitly blocked.

How it works

Configure the rules once and let Odoo filter products automatically during quotation creation.

1

Create Product Groups

Create groups such as regions, project types, customer catalog types or internal product categories.

2

Assign groups to products

Open product templates and assign the relevant Product Groups.

3

Configure pricelists

Set Allowed Product Groups, select a policy and optionally add Forbidden Products.

4

Sell with confidence

When a pricelist is selected on a quotation, the product selector only offers valid products.

Two levels of exclusions

Use broad group restrictions for structure and explicit exclusions for exceptions.

On the product

Excluded Pricelists lets you define pricelists where that product may never be selected.

  • Best for product-specific restrictions.
  • Useful when one product is not valid for one or more sales catalogs.
  • Easy to manage from the product form.

On the pricelist

Forbidden Products lets you block individual products directly from the pricelist.

  • Best for pricelist-specific exceptions.
  • Useful when a catalog excludes a small number of products.
  • Easy to manage from the pricelist form.
Recommended setup: use Product Groups for your main product availability rules, and use Forbidden Products or Excluded Pricelists only for exceptions. This keeps configuration simple and predictable.

Typical use cases

Regional product catalogs

Only show products that are available for a selected region or local project pricelist.

Customer-specific pricing

Control which products are valid for a customer or contract-specific pricelist.

Project-based sales

Separate products by project type while keeping a single shared product catalog in Odoo.
